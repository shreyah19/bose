About Us

BOSE AQUARIUM started selling across India to the whole seller and retailer of ornamental fresh water fish since 2014.
We collect fish from hatchery bidder, developer and also from our expert staffs. After that we place it within aquarium in fresh water and change the food habit to dry food and then we distribute the healthy fish to the customer allover India. We are ready to supply in bulk or small quantity including imported fish and all kind of fresh water fish.

Our Services

Maintenance

Good aquarium maintenance practices will lead to a healthy aquatic environment and thriving fish.

Health Services

Health service employs scientific methods to diagnose fish disease. Accurate diagnosis is the most important factor for treatment.

Unique Design

Fish Origin

We can provide fish from any country as per customer desire, and we manage the foreign fish health issues and food.

Aquarium Accessories

We provide unique aquarium accessories like Aquarium Air Stone, Aquarium Gravel Vacuum, Aquarium buckets etc.

Filtration
